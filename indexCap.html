Kiwi Fruit

One of the most underrated fruits of all time. This hairy sphere has a bautiful green flesh inside with a white center. Not only are kiwi fruits incredibly delicious. They are one of healthiest fruits in the world. A fruit that is actually deserving of its title as a superfood. Don't believe me? Well let me show you.

Kiwi fruit are loaded with all sorts of essential vitamins and minerals. To name a few ,Vitamins A, B6, B12, E, and potassium, calcium, iron and magnesium are all found found in kiwis, and in significant amounts. Did you know that kiwis actually have more vitamin C than oranges! Kiwi fruit are also a great source of fibre which aids in digestion. There are also certain antoxidants and enzymes that aid better sleep and improved digestion. Among all of these amazing health benefits, they also taste really great.

BlueBerries

Blueberries are an extremely healthy and tasty berry. They are packed with al sorts of vitamins, minerals and antioxidants

Blueberries have been found to reduce inflammation as well as oxidative stress. This is very important as both of those can be the cause of a wide range of diseases. BlueBerries can also lower cholesterol levels, promoting better heart health. BlueBerries are also high in fibre and improve insulin sensitivity.

Mexican Mince

This is a very tasty and versatile mince that can be eaten in a taco,burrito, torta or even by itself. It is a very easy recipe for those who are that confident in cooking. It is also great with sour cream! Serves 4.

  1. Brown a whole onion(diced) in a pot
  2. Add 500g mince and cook til brown
  3. Add a taco spice mix
  4. Add a cup of chopped tomatoes
  5. Add a cup of red beans
  6. Enjoy!

Chorizo dish

This a very delicious soup/stew with a great deal of flavour. It does take a while to make but boy is it worth it. It is a great dish to cook in bulk and save some for later, however with this meal leftovers are no guarantee!

  1. Brown a whole onion(diced) in a pot
  2. Add 3 cut potatoes and carrots
  3. Add chopped chorizo and bacon pieces
  4. Add lentils and beans
  5. Add spice
  6. Cook for an hour
